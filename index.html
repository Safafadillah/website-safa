About Me

Get to know me better

Hi! I’m Safa Fadillah a Front-End Developer and a student at SMK Wikrama Bogor, majoring in Software and Game Development. I have a strong passion for creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces. Most of my work focuses on front-end development, but I also have experience building web applications using Laravel.

My journey in the world of programming began in school and continued to develop through various projects, boot camps, and internship experiences. All of these experiences convinced me that web development was the right place for me.

I believe that great interfaces combine both functionality and aesthetics. That’s why I always aim to create designs that are clean, intuitive, and accessible to users. I enjoy solving problems, improving UI/UX, and continuously learning new technologies like Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Flutter (basic), and more.

Right now, I’m focusing on improving my front-end skills while continuing to build real projects that help me grow as a developer. I’m always excited to collaborate, learn, and create impactful digital experiences.
